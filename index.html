Famous Queso Dip

Recipe by Thomas Borzecki

  • Shopping List
  • Prepping
  • Directions
  • 1 pkg. (450 g) Velveeta Process Cheese Product, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 1 can (19 fl oz/540 mL) diced tomatoes, well drained
  • 1 can (4.5 oz/127 g) chopped green chiles, well drained
  • Prep Time - 10min.
  • Total Time 10min.
  • Servings - 28 servings, 2 Tbsp. (30 mL) each

This warm cheese dip is known for its simplicity - with only 3 ingredients and 10 minutes of prep, our Famous Queso Dip couldn’t be easier. It also couldn’t be tastier - it’s one of our favourite warm appetizer dips!

  • Combine ingredients in 2-L microwaveable bowl.
  • Microwave on HIGH 5 min. or until Velveeta is completely melted and mixture is well blended, stirring after 3 min.
